Shreyas Ghanathe

Founder of RevolutionaryRims

Shreyas Ghanathe

As a highly skilled and passionate professional bike restorer, I bring to the table a comprehensive set of skills that make me adept at breathing new life into bicycles of various types and eras. With an extensive background in mechanical engineering and a profound understanding of bicycle components, I excel in diagnosing and addressing mechanical issues, ensuring that every restored bike not only looks pristine but functions flawlessly. My expertise extends beyond mere repairs; I specialize in the meticulous restoration and refurbishment of vintage and classic bicycles. With a keen eye for detail, I've successfully undertaken numerous projects, reviving bikes to their original splendor through processes such as sanding, painting, and chrome plating. I take pride in preserving the authenticity of each bike, meticulously sourcing original parts to maintain historical accuracy. In addition to my technical skills, my problem-solving abilities have proven invaluable in navigating the challenges inherent in bike restoration. Whether it's locating rare replacement parts, addressing unforeseen issues, or customizing bikes to meet specific requirements, I approach each obstacle with creativity and precision, ensuring a seamless restoration process. My commitment to excellence is reflected not only in the bikes I restore but also in the relationships I build with clients. I understand the importance of customization, and I have the skills to modify bikes according to individual preferences or to enhance their overall performance. Having successfully restored and customized a diverse range of bicycles throughout my career, I am confident in my ability to deliver unparalleled craftsmanship. With a passion for preserving the heritage of cycling and a commitment to quality, I am eager to contribute my expertise to your team and further elevate the standards of bike restoration.
